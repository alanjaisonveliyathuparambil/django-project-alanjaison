ABOUT US

We Make Scrap Collection Simple

SwiftScrap is a digital Scrap Pickup Management System created to connect users, collectors, and administrators in one platform.


Users can schedule pickups, collectors can manage assigned pickups, and administrators can control scrap prices, requests, transactions, users, and reports.


The goal of SwiftScrap is to support responsible recycling, reduce waste, and make scrap disposal fast, transparent, and eco-friendly.
